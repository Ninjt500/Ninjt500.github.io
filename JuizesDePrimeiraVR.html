Juizes de Primeira VR

Juizes de Primeira VR is a Android VR game prototype heavly inspired by the Ace Attorney game series, where instead of beeing a defense lawyer you assume the role of judge, and have the task of paying attention to the arguments presented, accept or denying evidence as proof and giving your final veredict on each case.

This game was developed as a college project with a small team of colleagues.
