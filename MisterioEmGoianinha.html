Misterio em Goianinha

Misterio em Goianinha is a PC Desktop multiplayer cooperative horror game where your objective is to collect 10 newspapers scattered across the map while avoiding beeing catch by Chupa Cu, a creature from a brazilian meme which name translates to "Ass sucker". The game supports up to 4 players.

The base for this game was developed as a college project by me alone and transformed into a multiplayer afterwards.
