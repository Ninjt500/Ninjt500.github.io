Powerplant Simulator

Powerplant simulator is a web browser/PC desktop idle clicker game, where you click in a button to generate energy, which you can than use to upgrade your suply range, or sell it for money and use it to buy new generators and upgrades to speedup you energy generation.

This game was developed as a college project with a small team of colleagues using only Unity's visual scripting.
