Dracula VS Nosferatu

Dracula VS Nosferatu is a PC Desktop puzzle plataformer game prototype, where you control Dracula and have to use the tools and powers at your disposal to solve puzzles e reach the top of the castle, to reclaim the throne stolen by Nosferatu. This prototype presents 3 levels and all the of the game mechanics.

This game was developed as a college project by a small group of colleagues.
