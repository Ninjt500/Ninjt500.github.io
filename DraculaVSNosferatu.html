Dracula VS Nosferatu

Dracula vs. Nosferatu is a PC desktop puzzle platform game prototype where you control Dracula and have to use the tools and powers at your disposal to solve puzzles and reach the top of the castle to reclaim the throne stolen by Nosferatu. This prototype presents three levels and all of the game mechanics.

This game was developed as a college project by a small group of colleagues.
