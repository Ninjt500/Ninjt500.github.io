Tetradome

Tetradome is the first game I have released on the Steam platform; it's also the first game I've ever sold. It was developed by me alone in the course of 10 months during my free time. The game is a non-linear puzzle-solving experience set in a fantasy environment with stylized 3D pixel art graphics where your objective is to explore and figure out how to disable the magic dome keeping you trapped. The dome is formed by the 4 elements of nature, and solving each of their puzzles gets you closer to escaping.
