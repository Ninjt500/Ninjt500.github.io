Hunter's Quest

Hunter's Quest is a PC Desktop action RPG open world game I am working on alone. In it, you will control Daisy, a young woman who hunts monsters for money and has a dream of finding the dragon that attacked her village when she was young and gave her fire control powers. The game is set is a medieval fantasy world, with different biomes and dungeons filled with monsters. The gameplay focus are the combat, on ground or while flying, and the quest lines.

This game is beeing developed by me alone as a hobbie during my free time, and don't have any release date programmed. I have the intention of releasing it on the Steam platform when its ready.
