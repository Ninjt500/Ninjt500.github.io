Hunter's Quest

Hunter's Quest is a PC action RPG open-world game I am working on alone. In it, you will control Daisy, a young woman who hunts monsters for money and has a dream of finding the dragon that attacked her village when she was young and gave her fire control powers. The game is set in a medieval fantasy world with different biomes and dungeons filled with monsters. The gameplay focuses on grounded and airborne melee combat and on the quest lines.

This game is being developed by me alone as a hobby during my free time and doesn't have a release date programmed. I have the intention of releasing it on the Steam platform when it's ready.
