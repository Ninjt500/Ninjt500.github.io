Limpa Espirito Santo

Limpa Espirito Santo is an Android mobile educational arcade game where you visit 30 different stages, each one representing a Brazilian landmark in the state of Espirito Santo, and collect waste falling on the screen using the correct bin for each garbage type while also learning a bit about each place visited.

This game was developed by a team of two people with the support of the local emergency law "Aldir Blanc" and the secretary of culture of the state of Espirito Santo.
