About Me

My name is Tiago Denaday; username "Ninjt500" and currently publishing work under the name "Ninjt Productions". I was born in 2001 in Espirto Santo, Brazil, where I still live. My native language is Brazilian Portuguese, and I can also speak English.

I've graduated as an electrical technician from IFES Campus Vitória in 2019 and in Digital Games Technology from FAESA University in 2022.

I have always loved video games since I can remember, and in 2017, I first took an interest in learning game development. After completing high school in 2019, I went to college to learn how to make a living out of it. During college, in 2021, I started a remote part-time job at Inside Tecnologia, where I still work today, but now as a full-time junior developer.

I mostly focus on C# programming and the Unity editor, but I have also learned about Unity's shader graph, 3D modeling, rigging, skinning, animation, and 2D artwork. Skills that I often use in my projects.
