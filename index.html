About Me

My name is Tiago Barroso Denaday, I also go by the online username "Ninjt500", born in 2001 in Espirto Santo, Brazil, where I still live. My native language is brazilian portuguese and I can also speak english.

I've graduated as electrical technician in IFES Campus Vitória in 2019 and Digital Games Tecnology in FAESA University in 2022

I always loved Video Games since i can remember, and in 2017 I first took interest in learning game development. After completing high school in 2019, I went to college to learn how to make a living out of it. During college, in 2021, I started a remote part time job at Inside Tecnologia, where I work until today, but now as a full time junior developer.

I mostly focus in c# programming and unity editor, but I also studied shader graph, 3D modeling, rigging, skinning, animation and 2D artwork. Skills which I often use in my projects.
