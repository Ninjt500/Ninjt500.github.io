About Me

My name is Tiago Barroso Denaday, I also go by the online username "Ninjt500", born in 2001 in Espirto Santo, Brazil, where I still live.

I've graduated as electrical technician in IFES Campus Vitória in 2019 and Digital Games Tecnology in FAESA University in 2022

I always loved Video Games since i can remember beeing alive, and in 2017 I first took interest in learning game development and after completing high school in 2019, I went to college to learn to do it as my job. During college, in 2021, I started a remote part time job at Inside Tecnologia, where I work until today, but now as a full time junior developer.
